Die\Character commands can contain any of the following 4 elements. Order does not matter.

	[character ref] : Establishes which characters are associated with the roll\edit. 
		-It can be the entire party ("p", or "party") or specific names. 

	[die] : Specifies the die and how many times to roll it. Syntax:
		d[sides](*[rollcount])      () optional.

	[mod] : Adds some constant or character stat to roll result. Syntax:
		[operator][constant or stat](*)  () optional.

	[stat edit] : Edits each character's selected stat. Syntax:
		[stat][operator]
